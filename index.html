Our Mission

We aim to be THE meeting place of Sint-Niklaas. Our walk-in center 'DE INLOOP' provides a home for all, especially for our fellow citizens in (risk of) impoverished situations. We not only offer a listening ear and social and societal advice, but also support in meeting basic necessities of life.

Our Vision

We envision the Social Walk-In Center 'DE INLOOP' as a primary incubator where respect and equality are central. Everyone is welcome here, regardless of belief, worldview, political inclination, race, gender, or any other basis. We aim to provide everyone who enters our doors with the tools and necessary support to independently and confidently discover and pursue their unique path in the world.

Our Goals

We strive to create a platform that supports individuals to unlock their full potential. We provide not only essential items such as clothes,household goods, toys, and food, but also strive to create opportunities for growth and development. Our giveaway shop and coffee/lunch corner are the foundations of this mission, but we go further. We offer educational and language support to kids, youth and adults, organize creative activities for children to develop their skills, and promote social interaction through singing, dancing, and sports activities.

How You Can Help

You can contribute through financial support, by lending a hand in the renovation of our premises, by donating goods in good condition for our giveaway shop, or by offering your time and heart as a volunteer in our team of passionate and loving individuals.

  • Financial Contribution: Support us with your financial donations to enable our projects and activities.
  • Volunteer: Join our team and contribute your time and skills in various roles within our organization.
  • Donate Goods: Provide items in good condition for our giveaway shop to assist those in need.
  • Share Our Story: Help expand our reach by sharing our story within your network and community.

We are a non-profit organization, driven by the desire to be a beacon of hope in the lives of those facing hardship.
