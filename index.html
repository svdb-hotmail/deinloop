Your Support, Their Future

Social Walk-In Center ‘DE INLOOP’ in Sint-Niklaas

“Extending More Than Just a Hand—Creating Lifelong Impact”

Our Mission

Become the hub of Sint-Niklaas where everyone finds a sanctuary, particularly those at risk of poverty. We’re more than just a listening ear; we provide actionable social advice and resources to cover life’s essentials.

Our Vision

We see DE INLOOP as a transformative space, rooted in respect and equality. Our doors are open to everyone, irrespective of background or beliefs. Here, individuals gain the resources and support to carve out their own pathways in life.

Our Goals

Beyond offering essential goods like clothing and food, we aim to be a catalyst for personal growth and societal contribution. Our facilities, including an educational hub and activity center, foster skill development and community building. We’re more than a giveaway shop; we’re a lifeline for development and social interaction.

How You Can Help

  • Financial Contributions: Enable us to scale our impact.
  • Renovation Support: Help us create a space that reflects our values.
  • Item Donations: Enhance our giveaway shop’s offerings.
  • Volunteer: Join our passionate team to create direct impact.
